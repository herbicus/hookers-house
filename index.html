The Journey - Sonoma Valley


To get to where we are now, you must begin in Kentucky. We hand-select premium, never-blended single-barrel Bourbon that ages atleast 6 years in new oak barrels. With a high content of rye, the bourbon is spicier and a little bolder. After the initial aging, each barrel is transferred at barrel strength into a used Pinot Noir barrels from the Carneros wine region of Sonoma Valley. The French wine barrels were hand selected based on flavor profiles from the previous wine vintages by the head wine maker Mike Cox who had commissioned these barrels while visiting Burgundy, France.

The secondary aging (or doubling-barreling) imparts subtle flavors and nuisances into my Bourbon creating a one of kind refined balanced Bourbon.
